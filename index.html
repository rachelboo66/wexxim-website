About Wexxim

Two decades of milling Malaysian hardwood, kept in the family.

Since 2001, Wexxim Holdings has manufactured and supplied solid timber products from Malaysia to customers across local and international markets.

With integrated capabilities in sawmilling, kiln drying, and processing, we keep tight control over quality, consistency, and supply reliability — from raw log to finished board.

Over two decades of experience backs every order, with dependable timber and long-term partnerships built on trust and consistency.
